Privacy Policy

Introduction

Card Game is developed by Abbas Ali, a blind developer from Quetta, Pakistan. This app is designed with accessibility-first principles for blind and visually impaired users. Currently the app runs fully offline. Online multiplayer and additional features are planned for future versions.

Data Collection

Currently, this app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All game data including scores and settings is stored locally on your device only. This will be updated when online features are introduced.

Google Sign-In (Optional)

The app offers optional Google Sign-In for profile features. If you choose to sign in, only your Google display name and profile picture are used within the app. We do not access your email, contacts, or any other Google account data without your permission.

Notification Permission

This app will request permission to send you notifications. This permission is required to inform you about new game updates, features, and announcements. You can deny this permission and the app will still work normally. You can change this setting at any time in your device settings.

Future Permissions

In future versions, the app may request camera permission to allow you to select a custom profile avatar, and microphone permission to support voice chat in online multiplayer mode. These permissions will only be requested when those features are available and will always be optional.

Monetization

This app currently has no advertisements or in-app purchases. Future versions may include hybrid monetization such as optional cosmetic items or premium features. Any monetization will be clearly disclosed and will not affect core gameplay.

Sound & Storage

The app uses audio sound effects stored locally. All game preferences are saved on your device using browser local storage. No data leaves your device in the current offline version.

Children

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The games are suitable for all ages.

Contact

Developed by Abbas Ali — Blind Developer, Quetta, Balochistan, Pakistan. Developer contact details and social media links will be added in a future update.
